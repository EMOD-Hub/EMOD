========
Outbreak
========

The **Outbreak** class allows the introduction of a disease outbreak event by the addition of
infected individuals or by the infection of existing individuals in the specified node set. The type
of disease outbreak to be added must be configured. **Outbreak** is a node-level intervention; to
distribute an outbreak intervention to specific categories of individuals within a targeted node,
use **OutbreakIndividual**.
