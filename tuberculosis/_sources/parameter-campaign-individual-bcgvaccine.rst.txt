==========
BCGVaccine
==========

The **BCGVaccine** intervention class includes vaccination in the simulation. Vaccination can reduce
the likelihood of infection or transmission of pathogens. This intervention adds further
functionality to vaccination by including the ability to vary vaccine take with age.
