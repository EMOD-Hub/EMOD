===========================
HealthSeekingBehaviorUpdate
===========================

The **HealthSeekingBehaviorUpdate** intervention class dynamically updates the probability of care-
seeking behaviors. The initial probability of care is set in
:doc:`parameter-campaign-individual-healthseekingbehaviorupdateable`.
