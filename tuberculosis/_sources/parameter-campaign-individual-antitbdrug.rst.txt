==========
AntiTBDrug
==========

The **AntiTBDrug** intervention class specifies how TB drugs affect individuals in the model. After
treatment, individuals may achieve true cure, return to the latent state but quickly relapse, return
completely to the latent state, die during treatment, or fail treatment completely.
