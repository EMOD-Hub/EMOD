=================
MalariaDiagnostic
=================

The **MalariaDiagnostic** intervention class is similar to :doc:`parameter-campaign-individual-simplediagnostic`,
but distributes a test for malaria. There are three types of configurable diagnostics, each with their
own sensitivity parameters.
