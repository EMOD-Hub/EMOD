========
InputEIR
========

The **InputEIR** intervention class enables the Entomological Inoculation Rate (EIR) to be configured for
each month of the year in a particular node. The EIR is the number of mosquito bites per night
multiplied by the proportion of those bites that are positive for sporozoites.
