================
AntimalarialDrug
================

The **AntimalarialDrug** intervention is used to apply drug-based control efforts to malaria
simulations. When configuring this intervention, note that the **Malaria_Drug_Params** need to be
configured, as they govern how particular anti-malarial drugs will behave.
